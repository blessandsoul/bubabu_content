## Flag legend

- **impulse** — small low-price add-on / checkout-line item.
- **premium-gift** — 100₾+ category, lead with emotional gift-occasion not price.
- **barcode-pending** — placeholder barcode in catalog; confirm final product + assign real barcode before label print.
- **safety-review-pending** — small magnetic parts; needs kid-safety review before public ad / shelf display under Bubabu Smart Gifts.
- **sku-dup-tbc** — possibly same physical product as another SKU; confirm before stock count.
- **model-mismatch** — box vs invoice model number disagree; verify physical model before label print.
- **function-check-needed** — verify product function works as advertised before sale.
- **description-blank** — source row missing description; do not invent — pull from supplier.
- **age-6+** / **age-8+** / **age-9+** / **age-3+** — minimum age stated by source; honor in marketing copy.
- **DO-NOT-CONFLATE-WITH-BUBABU-HERO** — third-party musical-owl toy; never imply this IS Bubabu in any image / ad / caption.
- **price-pending** / **qty-pending** — new SKU from 2026-05-29 photo scan, awaiting Axiom Smart confirmation. NOT publishable in ad copy until cleared.
- **cozy** — Cozy mood-state SKU category (plush, bedtime, soft-hold gifts).
- **educational** — Educational zone SKU category (learning, early-literacy, STEM).
- **construction** — Construction zone SKU category (build-with-hands, STEM, magnetic-tiles).
- **premium-gift-addon** — accessory/refill that sells WITH a premium-gift parent SKU (e.g. 3D-pen filament with 3D-pen).
