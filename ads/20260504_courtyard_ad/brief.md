# Bubabu - Courtyard Ad "ეზოს ბიჭები" (Pixar 3D)

**Concept:** Boys zombie-scroll TikTok in Soviet courtyard → Mom calls from 6th floor → unboxing → Bubabu speaks → group joy
**Style:** Pixar 3D animation throughout (full AI generation)
**Pillar:** P2 (Pain) → P1 (Proof) → P5 (CTA)
**Duration:** ~70s master | 60s cut | 30s Reels cut
**Format:** 16:9 master → 9:16 crop
**Language:** Georgian dialogue
**Brief filed:** 2026-05-04

---

## Why Pixar 3D (not real footage)

| Reason | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Bypasses Imagen "minor" upload block | No content policy issues |
| Matches Bubabu plush aesthetic | Same DNA as the toy itself |
| Magic moment hits harder | Glowing AI toy in cartoon = natural, in real = uncanny |
| AI generators trained heavily on Pixar | Most stable, consistent output |
| No casting/location costs | 100% production cost = AI compute |
| Cross-promo with cartoon series | Bubabu has 3 active cartoon concepts - these characters can recur |
| Faster turnaround | Days vs weeks for live shoot |

## Why This Works (story mechanics)

| Mechanism | Execution |
|-----------|-----------|
| Pain before product | TikTok zombie boys → viewer says "that's my kid" |
| Mom as hero | Georgian mothers = trusted authority figure |
| Social proof built-in | 3 friends captivated = peer validation before first word |
| Product speaks first | Bubabu initiates → positions as alive, not a toy |
| Group joy | Laughing montage = universal desire to belong |
