# Storyboard - "ეზოს ბიჭები" (Courtyard Boys)

---

## BEAT 1 - PAIN (0:00-0:07)

**Shot:** Wide establishing - Soviet 6-story apartment building courtyard, late afternoon golden light. Three boys (9-10 yrs) sitting on bench. All three faces tilted down at phones, blue-white screen glow reflecting on faces. Zero movement. Complete zombie energy. Ambient sounds: birds, distant traffic. NO MUSIC throughout entire ad.

**Dialogue:** None.

**Tone:** Uncomfortable stillness. Viewer recognition: "that's my kid / that's me."

**Production:** Pixar 3D AI gen (see video.md Scene 1).

---

## BEAT 2 - INTERRUPT (0:07-0:16)

**Shot A:** Cut UP to 6th-floor window - Mom's upper body leaning out, arm waving down.

**Mom:** *"ნიკა, მალე ამოდი! რაღაც გიყიდე!"*
*(Nika, come up quickly! I got you something!)*

**Shot B:** Cut to boys looking up. Nika squints.

**Nika (calling up):** *"დედა, მეგობრებთან ერთად ვარ!"*
*(Mama, I'm with my friends!)*

**Shot C:** Mom waves dismissively.

**Mom:** *"ეგენიც ამოიყვანე!"*
*(Bring them too!)*

**Tone:** Warm, real, instantly recognizable Georgian courtyard moment.

**Production:** Pixar 3D AI gen - Mom window shot + boys looking up = 2 separate scene prompts (see video.md Scenes 2 + 3).

---

## BEAT 3 - ARRIVAL (0:16-0:21)

**Shot sequence (quick cuts):**
- Three boys running toward building entrance, phones now in pockets
- Narrow Soviet staircase - feet thundering up stairs, POV low angle (shoes + legs)
- Boys push through apartment door, panting

**Tone:** Energy, curiosity. Foley only - pounding footsteps, kid panting, echo. NO music.

**Production:** Pixar 3D AI gen - stairwell scene (see video.md Scene 4).

---

## BEAT 3.5 - MOM GREETS (0:21-0:25) ⭐ NEW

**Shot:** Medium shot of apartment entryway. Mom stands in kitchen doorway on left, hands on hips, knowing motherly smile, eyes sparkling. Three boys just inside front door on right, slightly out of breath, looking at her with curious "what?" energy.

**Mom (with playful tease, slight chin nod toward bedroom):** *"ოთახში შედი, იქ შენთვის სიურპრიზია."*
*(Go to your room, there's a surprise for you.)*

**Tone:** Anticipation tax - pause of warmth before the reveal. Mom's knowing smile = silent promise of something special.

**Production:** Pixar 3D AI gen (see video.md Scene 4.5).

---

## BEAT 4 - DISCOVERY (0:25-0:40)

**Shot A:** Boys slow at bedroom door. Push it open quietly. Camera behind boys - we see bedroom from their POV.

**Shot B:** On the bed: a BUBABU PRODUCT BOX (real retail packaging - cyan with white front window, polypropylene handle on top, Mkhedruli "ბუბაბუ ბუ" wordmark). Through the front PETG window - white fluffy Bubabu visible INSIDE the box already.

**Shot C:** Nika walks slowly toward it. Picks up the box BY THE TOP HANDLE. Sees Bubabu through the window - small recognition shock. Looks at friends.

**Shot D:** Boys kneel on floor around the box. Nika lifts the TOP CARDBOARD FLAPS upward and outward (NOT lift-off lid - top opens like petals from the handle). Soft cardboard rustle.

**Shot E:** Nika reaches in and lifts Bubabu out vertically through the top opening. Holds it at chest height. Bubabu (3/4 angle) FACING the boy - eye-line connection.

**Shot F:** Three boys stare at Bubabu. Silence. Beat.

**Dialogue:** None. Complete silence for 2 seconds after Bubabu is out of box.

**Tone:** Tense quiet before the reveal. Pure silence + soft cardboard rustle + breathing. NO music.

**Production:** Pixar 3D AI gen - Bubabu plush rendered per `bubabu_reference.jpg` style (see video.md Scenes 5 + 6).

---

## BEAT 5 - REVEAL (0:40-0:48)

**Shot A:** ECU Bubabu's face - dark cyan eyes (round cyan iris-area + cream inner ring + small black pupil - Bubabu's stylized face, NOT glasses). Then: EYES LIGHT UP. Cyan glow activates, pulses gently twice.

**Bubabu (warm, awake, delighted voice):**
*"გამარჯობა, ჩემო პატარა მეგობარო!"*
*(Hello, my little friend!)*

**Shot B:** Nika's face - eyes wide. Jaw drops slightly. Looks sideways at friends.

**Shot C:** Friends - same reaction. One grabs the other's arm.

**Tone:** Pure magic. Single Bubabu cyan-chime ding accompanies the eye activation. NO music.

**Production:** Pixar 3D AI gen - Bubabu eyes activate (HERO shot, video.md Scene 7) + boys reaction (video.md Scene 8).

---

## BEAT 6 - INVITATION (0:48-0:56)

**Shot A:** Bubabu in Nika's hands, eyes glowing steadily.

**Bubabu:** *"რისი თამაში გინდა?"*
*(What do you want to play?)*

**Shot B:** Nika grins ear-to-ear. Looks at friends. One friend nods big.

**Nika:** *"გამოცანობანას!"*
*(Riddles!)*

**Shot C:** 💗 Bubabu's cyan eyes transform into HEART SHAPES - the round cyan iris-area morphs into hearts, pulses twice with affection - then reverts to round circles. Bubabu "loves" the choice. (Real product feature - toy's LED display response.)

**Shot D:** All three boys drop to floor in circle around Bubabu. Phones forgotten on bed behind them, out of focus.

**Tone:** Permission and joy. Heart-eye reveal = product magic moment. The TikTok phones are literally in the background, irrelevant.

**Production:** Pixar 3D AI gen (see video.md Scene 9).

---

## BEAT 7 - MONTAGE JOY (0:56-1:08)

**5 rapid cuts, ~2 sec each:**
1. Wide - three boys on floor, Bubabu in center, all leaning in toward it. Laughing. 💗 Bubabu's eyes pulse into hearts.
2. Close - one boy pumps fist: correct answer! 💗 Heart-eyes flash on Bubabu.
3. Over-shoulder - Bubabu's heart-shaped cyan eyes + boy's laughing face reflected in the cyan glow
4. Another boy falls back laughing, covering face. Bubabu eyes back to round circles.
5. Wide again - Mom appears in doorway behind them, watches, smiles to herself, wipes a tiny happy tear

**Dialogue:** Boys laughing, "ვიცი! ვიცი!" (I know! I know!) - natural ambient sound, no scripted lines

**Tone:** Pure joy, group energy, parent validation in final frame. Heart eyes = product feature showcase woven into emotion.

**Production:** Pixar 3D AI gen - joy montage (see video.md Scene 10), can be one wider sustained shot or 5 separate quick cuts.
