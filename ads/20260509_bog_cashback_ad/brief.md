# Bubabu × Bank of Georgia Cashback Weekend — Meta Ads Spot

**Window:** May 9–10, 2026 (Saturday + Sunday only)
**Offer:** 30% cashback with Bank of Georgia card on bubabu.ge purchases
**Effective price:** 360 ₾ → **252 ₾** after cashback
**Brief filed:** 2026-05-04
**Style:** Pixar 3D + Candy Pop palette (consistent with `20260504_courtyard_ad/`)
**Language:** Georgian voiceover/text + EN/RU subtitles for expat targeting

---

## Mission

Meta Ads spot designed for **conversion + urgency**. Sound-off compatible (most FB/IG users mute). Big visible numbers, clear deadline, single CTA.

Primary metric: ROAS (return on ad spend) > 2.0× during weekend
Secondary: CTR > 2%, CPC < 1.5 ₾

---

## Format Specs

ONE 15-second master video, internally stitched from **3 micro-shots** (5s each) editor-composited into single timeline. Exported in 3 aspect-ratio crops for Meta placements:

| Crop | Use | Source |
|------|-----|--------|
| **9:16 1080×1920** ⭐ | IG Reels / Stories / TikTok / Audience Network | MASTER |
| **1:1 1080×1080** | IG Feed / FB mobile feed | Crop top + bottom 25% |
| **16:9 1920×1080** | FB desktop feed | Reframe Bubabu/child left + sticker stack right |

**3-shot structure** (per `video.md`):
- Shot A (0–5s) — Bubabu solo hero intro + 30% sticker pop
- Shot B (5–10s) — Georgian child holds Bubabu (use-case + scale anchor + emotional warmth)
- Shot C (10–15s) — Bubabu solo CTA close + price reveal + URL

Single 15s output — 3 internal shots stitched in editor with hard cuts (or 4-frame crossfade) at 0:05 and 0:10. Rationale: cold viewer needs use-case shot to understand "what is this thing" — pure-product void underperforms by 30-50% on conversion ROAS without human anchor.

## Visual Approach

- **Bubabu hero:** Pixar 3D plush ~20cm handheld scale, matte fabric face, printed cyan eyes (NOT LED, NOT glow). NO heart eyes — standard cyan only.
- **Big 30% sticker:** hot magenta circle stamp, slight tilt, "30%" + "ქეშბექი" in chunky white sans
- **Date band:** "9-10 მაისს" in lime-yellow gradient pill
- **Price reveal:** strikethrough 360₾ → big 252₾ in magenta
- **Calendar visual:** small 2-day calendar highlighting Sat-Sun
- **Bank of Georgia card icon:** subtle bottom-corner brand co-presence
- **CTA:** "bubabu.ge" lime-orange pill bottom

Background: butter-cream Candy Pop with sunburst rays, 3D confetti.

## Voiceover

Single Georgian female warm voice (Sulafat baseline from `voiceover.md`). Short hook + price + URL. Sound-off compatible — burned-in Georgian captions match VO word-for-word.

EN/RU subtitle tracks toggleable for expat audience targeting.

## Targeting (Meta Ads)

- **Geo:** Georgia + neighbouring CIS expats
- **Age:** 25–55 mothers + 35–65 grandparents
- **Interests:** kids toys, parenting, AI, edutainment, Bank of Georgia Bank
- **Behaviour:** clicked Bubabu before / visited bubabu.ge / interacted with previous Bubabu posts (warm retargeting)
- **Languages:** Georgian primary, Russian secondary, English tertiary

## Production Pipeline

| Step | Tool | Output |
|------|------|--------|
| 1. Bubabu hero still | Nano Banana 2 / Midjourney | 1 hero frame (clean, no text) |
| 2. Img2vid animation | Veo3 / Kling 2.0 | 1× 15s clip |

| 4. Voiceover | Gemini TTS Sulafat | 1× ~12s Georgian audio |
| 5. Captions | manual SRT | Georgian primary + RU/EN toggle |
| 6. Composite + master | CapCut / After Effects | Final 15s with overlays + VO + SFX |
| 7. Crops | Premiere/CapCut | 9:16 master + 1:1 + 16:9 |
