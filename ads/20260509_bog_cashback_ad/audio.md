# Audio Design - Bank of Georgia Cashback Ad (15s)

**Per AUDIO_ENGINE v1.0** - paid Meta Ads spot, sound-off compatible.
**Format:** SFX-driven sound design + warm narrator VO. **NO MUSIC** by design (paid ad ducks dialogue, licensing risk, sound-off context).
**Genre:** Premium commercial sound design (Prod-family, client-specific) - signature cyan-chime architecture replaces melodic track.
**Track length:** 15s (VO ~12s + 1.5s intro chime + 1s outro chime).

---

## STYLE FIELD (Suno N/A - this is editor sound design, not Suno music)

```
Premium kids-commercial sound design, sound-off compatible Meta Ads spot, NO MUSIC LAYER by deliberate creative choice — signature Bubabu cyan-chime functions as the emotional anchor in place of melodic music. Bright Candy Pop sonic palette in implied C major tonality (cyan-chime ascending C5→E5 bell tone, 0.8s decay with soft attack and tape-warm bloom, the brand's signature ear-worm). Light foley world: warm cozy room tone bed at -30dB throughout, suggesting butter-cream nursery-commercial space, no traffic, no echo, no exterior atmosphere. Rhythmic SFX punctuation lands on each visual beat: elastic pop on 30% sticker entry, strikethrough swoosh on price crossout, bouncy elastic boing on 252 ₾ landing, soft swoosh on CTA pill rise, signature chime closes the loop. Equivalent BPM 100 (energetic playful but not frantic — matches Voice A/B/C bouncy commercial pacing). Voiceover dominates middle frequency range — Sulafat warm Georgian female mom-energy at -12dB front, every SFX cleared 300Hz-3kHz to keep voice crystal at sound-off → sound-on transition. Emotional arc: 0-1s curiosity-open with cyan-chime ear-catch → 1-4s warm intimate setup over Sulafat hook line "მხოლოდ შაბათ-კვირას" → 4-8s price-drop punctuation peaks (swoosh + elastic boing land 360 strikeout and 252 reveal) → 8-12s confident close over CTA pill rise → 12-14s clean breath-room with room tone only → 14-15s signature cyan-chime closes loop, brand-recall outro. Sync rule: every SFX hits within 2 frames (80ms) of visual sticker entry, EVERY beat point of voiceover landed on by SFX or chime. The cyan-chime IS the brand sound — ear-worm replacing melodic hook. The happiness comes from rhythmic cleanliness, not from layered orchestration.
```

**Char count:** ~1,180 (within AE§10 800-1200 range).

## SILENCE PLAN (AE§5)

**ONE intentional silence point: 0:12-0:14 (2 seconds, room tone only).**

Function: lets `bubabu.ge` URL linger in viewer's mind after VO ends. AE§5 rule "music DROPS so words HIT" applied - here SFX drops so the URL HITS. The 2s breath = the strongest URL recall trigger of the whole spot.

No silence inside the 0:00-0:12 active block - paid ad cannot afford dead air during pitch.

## SOUND-OFF STRATEGY

80% Meta Ads viewers mute. Audio is bonus, captions + stickers carry message.
- Burned-in Mkhedruli captions (per `captions.md`)
- Big "30%" magenta sticker
- Big "252 ₾" price reveal
- Big "9-10 მაისს" date band
- Big "bubabu.ge" URL pill

Sound-on bonus: cyan-chime is the brand earworm - viewer who unmutes once associates the chime with Bubabu forever. Repeat exposure = brand-recall multiplier.

## PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

1. Generate VO in Gemini AI Studio per `voiceover.md` (Sulafat, ~12s)
2. Synthesize cyan-chime (custom synth - C5→E5 bell, 0.8s decay, soft attack, tape-warm bloom)
3. Pull other SFX from library (elastic pop, swoosh, boing, CTA swoosh)
4. Source warm room tone (nursery-commercial space, -30dB bed)
5. Edit timeline in CapCut/Premiere - VO + SFX + room tone per beat-point map above
6. Burn captions per `captions.md` (KA primary, RU/EN toggle)
7. Master per platform LUFS
8. Export 9:16 master + 1:1 + 16:9 crops
