## 1 · Brief
Bespoke score for one child's two-week journey from screen-stillness to building-wonder. NOT a reused template. A warm Pixar bedtime-daytime piece that starts soft and a little sleepy (the builder asleep), then warms instrument by instrument as the brain wakes, blooming brightest at day fifteen, and closing on the same hummable motif it opened with - the loop the viewer feels.

## 2 · THE MOTIF - "the wonder motif"
Bubabu brand motif: **C-E-G-E**, a 4-note hummable phrase (here voiced in G major as **G-B-D-B**). Opens the track on soft music-box celeste, alone and gentle. Recurs transformed at the day-fifteen payoff (lifted to D major, full warmth), and **bookends** the close on solo celeste again - same notes, warmer weight. A child should hum it after one watch. Arpeggio beds and pad washes are texture, NOT the motif.
