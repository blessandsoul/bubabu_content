Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe for children?

Yes. Answers are filtered and child-safe, there's no screen, and you configure themes and usage time in the parental app.

What age is it suitable for?

From age 3. Controls are voice-based, child doesn't need to know how to read.

What languages does Bubabu speak?

Georgian and English. Russian support will appear in the next update.

Is internet required?

Yes, Wi-Fi connection is required for smart responses. Basic games work offline.

How long does the battery last?

Up to 8 hours of active communication. Charges via USB-C in about 2 hours.
