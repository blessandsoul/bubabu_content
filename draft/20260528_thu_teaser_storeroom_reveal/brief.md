# Brief — Storeroom Reveal Teaser (Bubabu Smart Gifts launch)

## Concept

Brand-launch teaser for the new Bubabu Smart Gifts pivot. Bubabu in his cloud-tree hollow opens a door that was always there but never used — reveals his storeroom: shelves carved from the tree's own bark, lined with educational and magical wonder-gifts, and at the heart of the room an ancient wooden chest. Bubabu lifts the lid, the room fills with soft light. A guiding star drifts in. Bubabu lifts the first gift, tucks it into his wing. Closing image: Bubabu at the branch edge, paper-glider boat ready behind him. The promise: **soon, one child will wake up and find a gift on the windowsill.**

This is NOT a paired cartoon+ad (no specific catalog SKU featured). It's a standalone series-intro teaser that establishes the storeroom canon visually before any individual SKU pair ships. After this teaser, every cartoon+ad pair grows from the world it sets up.

## Goal

- Plant the storeroom canon in audience mind so future cartoon+ad pairs feel native, not bolted on.
- Build anticipation («soon... very soon...» — no specific date, just promise).
- Visually showcase «не простые игрушки, развивающие и магические» (per user phrasing) — shelves of items differentiated by mood-state (think / build-and-brave / cozy bedtime).
- Preserve every Bubabu lock: silent (beak closed), narrator-only (Charon Storytime), Pixar 3D not photoreal, cloud-tree origin canon v3, no Russian/Cyrillic in cartoon, no screen-tech vocabulary.

## Format

- ONE 60-90 sec narrator-led cartoon master, ADV mode v4 spec.
- 14 scenes per Bubabu ADV scene-count floor (matches Remedy 14-beat minimum).
- Standalone single-folder (NOT pair-folder structure — no companion ad needed).
- Slug: `20260528_thu_teaser_storeroom_reveal`.
- Structure borrowed from /remedy agent (per user directive — «возьми структуру у remedy»): voiceover-first pipeline, 14 beat-blocks, characters silent, narrator carries everything, foley-only video prompts.

## Three Bubabu values reflected

Each value-spark gets one shelf:
- **Curiosity (cyan)** → «gifts for children who like to think» — Wonder Canvas, puzzles, magic painting boards.
- **Courage (coral)** → «gifts for those who like to build and to brave» — magnetic builders, LEGO-style, paper-glider's little brother.
- **Kindness (gold)** → «gifts to hold close at bedtime» — Listening Shells, plush, Star Pebbles.

The chest at heart of room = the first gift Bubabu will bring. Symbol of the launch.
