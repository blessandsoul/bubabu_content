# Stand Text Stickers - 9 category labels (GE + EN separate) - 2026-06-12

## Client brief (Archil, verbatim scope)
- Stickers glued on the stand as category markers.
- White background. **Letters only** (no plates, no icons, no mascot - this brief supersedes the 2026-05-31 mascot+pill unification for THIS set; that lock stays for full-graphic sticker sets).
- Bold / shadow etc = designer's call.
- Georgian and English = **separate stickers** (18 total).
- GE letter height ~6-7 cm; length = whatever the text needs (every sticker different width).
- EN letter height ≈ half of GE (~3.2 cm caps).
- Client asks max contrast - suggested black on white.

## Design verdict (visible from distance + beautiful)
1. **Letters = pure black `#000` (print 100% K), Noto Sans Georgian.** Black-on-white = 21:1 contrast, nothing beats it at distance. Client is right - do not fight it. Weight: ~~900 Black~~ → ~~700 Bold~~ → **600 SemiBold default** (rev 2026-06-12 ×2: client thinning requests); preview ships a 400-900 switcher - final weight = client visual pick, recommended floor 500 (below that the 32 mm EN caps wash out under LED at distance). Thinning opens letter counters = cleaner read, but stroke must stay ≥ ~4 mm on EN.
2. **NO shadow, NO outline.** On a white background a shadow blurs the letter edge = WORSE legibility from afar. Shadow only helps on busy/photo backgrounds. Weight does the work.
3. ~~Mkhedruli~~ **REV 2026-06-12: MTAVRULI (uniform letter height)** - client follow-up directive «all letters one height». Every GE letter = exactly 65 mm, no ascenders/descenders; EN switched to ALL CAPS 32 mm to match the uniform system. This explicitly overrides the default mkhedruli-only sign rule for this text-sticker set (client/user verbatim > system default). Bonus: strips get shorter (≈11 cm GE / 6 cm EN) and shelf-line alignment is perfectly even.
4. **Candy Pop rainbow underline bar - IN** (killed by «ყველაფერი შავად», then restored same day «верни цвет линию»). Thin 6-color gradient bar (`#5BC0DE → #FF1F8F → #84CC16 → #FFEB3B → #FF9F1C → #FF6B6B`, 9 mm GE / 5 mm EN) under each inscription. Letters stay 100% black - the bar is the only color element, ties the set to the kiosk rainbow signature.
5. ~~Sticker #1 brand word in magenta~~ - **KILLED 2026-06-12**, black like everything else.
6. ~~Sticker #9 აქცია in signal red~~ - **KILLED 2026-06-12**, black like everything else.
7. **Matte lamination mandatory** - kiosk LED gloss hotspot kills distance legibility more than any color decision (carried from 20260531 print spec).

## Variants - RESOLVED 2026-06-12
Final combo: **black letters + rainbow underline**. «ჯობია ყველაფერი შავად ეწეროს» killed the colored WORDS (magenta brand word + red აქცია - stay dead); «верни цвет линию» restored the underline bar. So: all letters 100% K, the Candy Pop bar under each inscription = the single color element. Toggle in preview stays for final client sign-off.

## Size reality check (flag to client before print)
At 6.5 cm letter body height, long lines get LONG: sticker #1 ≈ ~1.8-1.9 m wide, #7 ≈ ~1.5 m. Exact widths auto-measured in `stickers_preview.html` labels. If the stand fascia is narrower - drop letter height for the long ones or confirm placement first.
