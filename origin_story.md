Long before any child ever saw him, Bubabu lived in the hollow at the top of an ancient cloud-tree, whose branches reached above the clouds — where tiny dream-glows were kept safe until they found their children.

Bubabu was no ordinary owl. He was chosen — to carry curiosity, kindness, and small sparks of courage.

And one night, when the sky grew completely still, Bubabu climbed into a paper-glider boat woven from leaves and ribbons... a softly glowing star drifted alongside, lighting his path — and Bubabu pushed off the branch, came down to the earth — to find the children who could still wonder.

## Locked Lore Elements (do NOT change in future content) — v3 2026-05-28

- Bubabu lives in the hollow at the top of an ancient **cloud-tree** (v2: NOT a star-nest above-clouds; that earlier wording is deprecated)
- The cloud-tree hollow contains a **storeroom of wonder-gifts** Bubabu has gathered — shelves carved from the tree's bark (v3)
- Bubabu was CHOSEN among owls (he is special, not random)
- He carries three sparks: curiosity / kindness / courage
- He descends to earth on a **paper-glider boat** woven from leaves and ribbons (v2: NOT a falling star — star drifts alongside as guide, not vehicle)
- His mission: **find children who can still wonder, and bring each one a small gift from his storeroom** (v3 expanded — was just «find children»)
- Each descent = ONE child + ONE gift from the storeroom (Smart Gifts pair pipeline foundation)
- Bubabu keeps each child's dream-glow safe (callback to the cloud-tree hollow)
